About Me

I'm a certified Transformational Life Coach, EFT, NLP Practitioner, Clinical Hypnotherapist & Stage IV Cancer Survivor celebrating four years NED. I was diagnosed with Colon Cancer in December 2017 completely out of the blue at just 31 years old, four months before my wedding.
What followed next was a whirlwind of decisions, rounds of chemotherapy and mental and spiritual trauma.
My own cancer journey and experiences as well as my training means that I am uniquely able to understand what you are going through to help you on your journey navigating life after diagnosis with a positive mindset.
