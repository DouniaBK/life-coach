The Six Pillars of Cognitive Power

Discover the power of anchoring and cognitive behavioural techniques by immersing yourself in guided meditation audios, and explore self-care practices such as Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT) using the six pillars of cognitive power.

Clarity:
Together, we'll create a roadmap to help you understand where you are now and where you want to be. Through self-assessment exercises and coaching sessions, you'll gain a holistic view of your life and identify areas that require attention and growth.


Mindset and Motivation:
Uncover and overcome your limiting beliefs while establishing clear goals for the future. Our coaching sessions and guided meditations will empower you to embrace a mindset that fuels motivation and propels you towards your aspirations.


Finding Peace and Freedom:
Release stress and anxiety, equipping yourself with tools to navigate life's challenges. Our coaching sessions and transformative NLP and EFT techniques will empower you to face any situation with grace and resilience.


Self-love and Body Image:
Embrace your body, scars, and all, fostering self-acceptance and surrendering to your diagnosis. Through self-study and guided hypnosis, you'll learn the importance of self-care and celebrate the beautiful person you are.


Connection and Communication:
Enhance your ability to express your thoughts and emotions effectively. We'll guide you through various techniques such as scripting, affirmations, and meditation to establish a deep connection between your mind, body, and spirit.


Create a Life You Love:
Reflect on your progress, set future goals, and ensure you're on the path to realising your dream life. Our coaching sessions will provide accountability and support as you create a future filled with joy, purpose, and fulfilment.
